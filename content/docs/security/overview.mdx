This section is the security model, the threats Olympus defends against, the mechanisms doing the defending, and the headers, encryption, and policies in effect.

## Defining the perimeter

- [Threat model](/docs/security/infrastructure/threat-model), what we defend against and what we don't.
- [CIAM/IAM isolation](/docs/security/infrastructure/ciam-iam-isolation), the rationale and the enforcement boundary.

## At rest

- [Encryption at rest](/docs/security/encryption-keys/encryption-at-rest), AES-256-GCM with HKDF-SHA256.
- [Encryption key blocklist](/docs/security/encryption-keys/encryption-key-blocklist), known-weak values the SDK refuses.

## In flight

- [Session cookies](/docs/security/identity-protection/session-cookies), HMAC-signed, separate `SESSION_SIGNING_KEY` from `ENCRYPTION_KEY`.
- [Security headers](/docs/security/infrastructure/security-headers), HSTS, X-Frame-Options, Referrer-Policy.
- [CSP, Athena](/docs/security/web-attacks/csp-athena), admin dashboard CSP.
- [CSP, Hera](/docs/security/web-attacks/csp-hera), login UI CSP.

## Abuse mitigation

- [Brute-force protection](/docs/security/web-attacks/brute-force), login-attempt tracking and account lockout.
- [Account lockout](/docs/security/web-attacks/account-lockout), Hera's lockout policy.
- [Breached password](/docs/security/identity-protection/breached-password), HaveIBeenPwned k-anonymity check.
- [Captcha Turnstile](/docs/security/web-attacks/captcha-turnstile), Cloudflare Turnstile policy.
- [Rate limiting](/docs/security/web-attacks/rate-limiting), Caddy `rate_limit` module.

## OAuth2 hardening

- [PKCE enforcement](/docs/security/identity-protection/pkce-enforcement), RFC 9700 alignment; mandatory for all public clients.

## Email and IdP trust

- [Email verification](/docs/security/identity-protection/email-verification), mandatory in production.
- [OIDC `email_verified` trust](/docs/security/identity-protection/oidc-email-verified-trust), why we never trust the upstream `email_verified` claim.

## DBA access

- [pgAdmin SSO](/docs/security/infrastructure/pgadmin-sso), OIDC through Olympus IAM Hydra.
- [pgAdmin DBA accounts](/docs/security/infrastructure/pgadmin-dba-accounts), least-privilege per-DBA database roles.
