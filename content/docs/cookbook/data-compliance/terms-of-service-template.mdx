## Account creation

By creating an account, you represent that:
- You are at least 16 years old (or 13 with parental consent where permitted).
- The information you provide is accurate.
- You are authorized to use the email address you provide.
- You will not create more than one account except as permitted by us.
- You will not impersonate others.

## Account security

You are responsible for:
- Maintaining the confidentiality of your password and second-factor devices.
- All activity that occurs under your account.
- Notifying us immediately of any unauthorized use.

We strongly recommend:
- Using a strong, unique password (we check against known breaches).
- Enabling two-factor authentication.
- Logging out of shared devices.

We may suspend accounts that show signs of compromise or that fail security checks.

## Acceptable use

You agree NOT to:
- Attempt to access accounts that aren't yours.
- Probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of our systems (unless under our published bug bounty program).
- Bypass rate limits, CAPTCHAs, or other security measures.
- Use automated tools (scrapers, bots) to interact with the service except via our public API with valid credentials.
- Reverse-engineer, decompile, or otherwise extract source code.
- Use the service to send spam, phishing, or malware.
- Use the service for any unlawful purpose.

Violations may result in immediate account termination.

### By you
You may delete your account at any time via account settings.

Upon termination, your access is revoked immediately and data is retained per the privacy policy.
