[SMTP2GO](https://smtp2go.com) is another solid transactional email option, with free tier (1000 emails/month) and decent global delivery infrastructure.

## Configuration

```yaml
courier:
  smtp:
    connection_uri: smtps://<smtp_user>:<smtp_password>@mail.smtp2go.com:465
    from_address: noreply@your-domain.com
    from_name: Your App
```

SMTP credentials are in the SMTP2GO dashboard → Settings → SMTP Users.

For Daedalus: choose **SMTP2GO** in the Email wizard; paste credentials.

## Free tier

- 1,000 emails/month
- All transactional
